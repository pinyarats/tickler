Tickler is your parking tickets handler.

A parking ticket management system which you can add, pay and debate your tickets. It also reminds you of the payment deadlines.


Three Simple Steps How Tickler Works

1. Add your ticket

So you get your parking ticket, and you are fustrated. Worry no more, Tricker is here to help! Simply take a photo of the ticket and Tickler automatically detects all the vital information and stored it as a new ticket for you.

2. Pay or Deabte

Simply click Pay on the selected ticket and Tickler automatically redirects you to payment page, enter credit card information and it's done. Feeling unjust? Hit Debate and input your reasons for debating, Tickler sends the form to the authority via our fast and reliable mail service.

3. Notify

On your own settings, Tickler can remind you when your ticket deadline is approaching, to help you avoid enormous additional fee for late payment. Options include by text messaging, email or simply on-device notifications.
